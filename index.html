FAQ

The questions everyone actually asks.

Will my prospects know it's automated?
No. Voice Match mirrors your exact writing style and the follow-up sends from your actual Gmail address. Your prospects think you wrote it at your desk. You did not. We did.
What sales strategies does the AI use?
RevLoop's AI is trained on frameworks tested across thousands of real salespeople and the proven strategies of the world's top sales closers — including follow-up sequencing, objection handling timing, and re-engagement psychology. Every follow-up is built on what actually works, not what sounds polite.
Does it send without me approving?
Only if you want it to. By default every draft lands in your Loop Queue for one-click approval. Flip on AutoLoop when you're ready to go fully hands-off.
What if a prospect replies mid-sequence?
The moment a reply lands, RevLoop detects it and immediately cancels all remaining steps. The deal moves back to Active automatically. No awkward double follow-ups — ever.
What's an Evergreen Loop?
A long-term sequence for prospects who need more time. Someone said "not right now" in February? RevLoop checks back in August — and again next February. The loop stays open until the deal is won or you say stop.
How is this different from a CRM?
A CRM tracks deals. RevLoop recovers them. No data entry, no pipeline maintenance. It sits on top of Gmail and works with what's already there. Setup in 3 minutes — not 3 days.
